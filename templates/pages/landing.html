About RankPulse

RankPulse is a comprehensive traffic administration platform built for digital marketing professionals, agencies, and businesses that need to manage organic and paid traffic at scale.

We integrate directly with the Google Ads API, Meta Marketing API, Google Analytics 4, Google Search Console, and OpenAI to provide a unified workspace for campaign management, SEO optimization, content generation, and performance reporting.

Our mission is to simplify multi-channel traffic management by consolidating all essential tools into one secure, efficient platform — so you can focus on scaling results instead of switching between dashboards.

Platform Highlights

  • Multi-account MCC support for managing multiple clients
  • OAuth 2.0 secure integration — no passwords stored
  • SSL/TLS encryption with HSTS for all traffic
  • LGPD & GDPR compliant data handling
  • AI-powered content generation and SEO recommendations
